Welcome to RUPP University

The Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP) stands as a beacon of academic excellence and innovation. Established in 1960, we are committed to nurturing the brightest minds and shaping the future leaders of Cambodia through comprehensive education, cutting-edge research, and a transformative learning experience.

Introduction RUPP

The Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP) is Cambodia's oldest and largest university, established in 1960. It plays a crucial role in the development of higher education in the country. RUPP offers a diverse range of programs across various fields, including social sciences, humanities, and natural sciences. The university is committed to providing quality education and fostering research that contributes to national development. With a focus on innovation and academic excellence, RUPP aims to prepare students for the challenges of the modern world. The university also emphasizes community engagement and strives to enhance the social well-being of the Cambodian population. RUPP's mission is to promote a culture of learning and critical thinking among its students, empowering them to become responsible citizens and leaders in their respective fields. The university's diverse academic environment encourages collaboration and the exchange of ideas, making it a vibrant hub for intellectual growth and development.

History RUPP

RUPP has a rich history that reflects the socio-political changes in Cambodia. Initially founded as a response to the need for higher education, the university has undergone significant transformations since its inception. During the Khmer Rouge regime, the university faced closure, and many intellectuals were persecuted. However, after the fall of the regime, RUPP was reestablished in 1993 and has since played a pivotal role in rebuilding the country's educational landscape. The university has expanded its programs and facilities, adapting to the evolving needs of society. Today, RUPP is recognized as a leading institution in Cambodia, contributing to the development of human resources and providing a platform for academic collaboration both nationally and internationally. The university's resilience and commitment to education continue to inspire generations of students. As it moves forward, RUPP remains dedicated to enhancing its academic offerings and fostering a spirit of inquiry and innovation among its students.

Major IT

The Information Technology (IT) program at RUPP is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving tech landscape. The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including software development, network management, cybersecurity, and data analysis. Students engage in hands-on projects and collaborate with industry partners to gain practical experience. The program emphasizes critical thinking and problem-solving skills
